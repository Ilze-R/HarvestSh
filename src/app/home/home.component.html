2023 Harvest Share

Let's come together and make a difference through the 2023 harvest share. Whatever you have or want, giving and receiving is one of the greatest joys about sharing. And Harvest Share is the link between something someone has and something someone wants. Your contributions will not only upgrade your karma, feed those in need but also promote sustainability and a more mindful approach to our food consumption.

How does it work?

Our community is the hub of connectedness. It’s helping reduce wastage and bringing people closer together to share their passion of food and gardening. You can connect to find and share fresh seasonal produce, plants. What’s more, once you’re connected with someone, you can even chat about tips and tricks of growing and making things, share your success stories in forum and find like minded people.
